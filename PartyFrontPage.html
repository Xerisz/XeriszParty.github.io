What's Freshers party you asked?



Every year when the first semester start we welcome you (freshers) to our school with an unforgettable party. Just like previous years, there will be crazy activities, delicious food, drink and most importantly we let freshman become more familiar with their future classmates and their seniors, which is also our main purpose of this welcome party. Sooo.....what are you waiting for?




What's there at the party?




Music

Enjoy all type of music in our school from your seniors. Rock, Pop, Classical, Vietnamese or Foreign music,... we have it all. And in the end, if you have interest in any of them, you can ask to join their club right away.



Foods

There will be lots of Food and Drink. We wouldn't let our juniors get hunry from all the party's activities. From snacks to fast foods, soft drinks to alcohol, we have them all. Some will be free and some will cost, so remember to bring CASH.



Activities

Beside from the music show, multiple Minigames are going to be hosted during the party, try your best to win some prizes. Also, at the end of the party, there will be a secret activity. So stay with us till the end if you want to join.
